Apply to Attend the 2016 CUWiP at UTSA/SwRI

Application

To attend the conference, you must first apply throug​h the central APS CUWiP application site.

Application period: September 1 to October 16, 2015

Registration for the conference can only happen aft​er you have received a notice from the APS that y​ou have been accepted.

You will be informed of your acceptance by the middle of November 2015. When you send in your application, it will be evaluated by the local organizing committee of the conference to which you applied (i.e. the conference that is closest to your anticipated location, January 15, 2016).

Note: This is a conference for undergraduate students. Strong preference will be given to physics majors (if you haven't declared your major but are seriously considering physics you may still apply).


Registration

Once you are accepted, you will be asked to register, also through a central APS site. Upon registration you will be asked to pay a $25 registration fee.

Lodging will be provided for students who are not local. Meals will be provided by the conference organizers. We encourage you to ask your department to help you with travel expenses. However, we can offer some travel assistance if necessary. Please see the Travel and Accomodations page for more information.

When you register, you will be asked if you are interested in presenting a research poster. Presenting a poster is not a requirement for attending the conference, but we do encourage all students who have been involved in research to bring a poster.

Conferences are supported in part by the National Science Foundation (PHY-1346627) and by the Department of Energy Office of Science (DE-SC0011076).
