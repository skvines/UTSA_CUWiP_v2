January 15 - 17

Welcome to the 2016 APS CUWiP at UTSA & SwRI

APS Conferences for Undergraduate Women in Physics (CUWiP) are three-day regional conferences for undergraduate physics majors.
View our conference flyer.

Apply Now

Goals of the Conference

Networking

Provide networking opportunities for undergraduate women.

Opportunities

Disseminate information about graduate schools and career opportunities.

Mentoring

Help undergraduate women connect with potential female mentors.

Support

Give undergraduate women the support they need to strive and be successful.

Locations

The 2016 conferences will be held at the following locations:

  •  Black Hills State University
  •  Georgia Institute of Technology
  •  Old Dominion University/Jefferson Lab
  •  Ohio State University
  •  Oregon State University
  •  Syracuse University
  •  University of California, San Diego
  •  University of Texas, San Antonio/Southwest Research Institute
  •  Wesleyan University

Conferences are supported in part by the National Science Foundation (PHY-1346627) and by the Department of Energy Office of Science (DE-SC0011076).
